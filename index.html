Time Management!

At work it isn't unusual that I have to either drive out to a location or work on something in the office. Due to this, it is important that I am constantly doing homework outside of work to balance this. To ensure that I have time for this, I found that going to work on days that I don't have class end up letting me do homework on my days off and create a better schedule for me.
